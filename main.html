Inception - A Christopher Nolan Film.

Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film written and directed by Christopher Nolan, who also produced the film with Emma Thomas, his wife. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious, and is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious.

Actor

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer
  • Marion Cotillard as Mal Cobb
  • Michael Caine as Professor Stephen Miles
